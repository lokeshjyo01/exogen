Decentralized Cryptocurrency Exchanges (“DEX”)

Decentralized exchanges operate on smart contracts, self-executing pieces of code on a blockchain. These smart contracts enhance privacy and reduce slippage (transaction costs) compared to centralized cryptocurrency exchanges.

However, since DEXs lack an intermediary third party, users need to navigate the platform independently, making DEXs more suitable for sophisticated investors.
