Data Collection:

We value the importance of safeguarding your privacy. We have implemented measures to ensure that we only collect information from you that is essential for providing our services. We are responsible for the collection, utilization, and retention of personal information in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act and relevant legal standards.

Types of Information Collected:

Upon visiting our platform, non-personal information such as your Internet Protocol ("IP") address, operating system, browser type, and Internet service provider may be collected. This information does not identify you personally. When registering on the platform, personal information like your name, mailing address, email address, phone/mobile number, and other relevant details may also be collected. We may obtain additional information, including geolocation data, in order to tailor advertisements and information based on user locations.

Handling of Social Logins:

In the context of our Services, should you choose to register or sign in via a social media account, certain information about you may come under our purview.

Our Services offer the functionality for users to register and sign in through third-party social media platforms using their account credentials (such as Facebook or Twitter logins). Upon doing so, we gain access to specific profile details about you from the social media provider. The nature of the profile information received may vary between social media providers, encompassing details like your name, email address, friends list, profile picture, and any other information you opt to share on the respective social media platform.

Any information obtained will be used strictly for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as explicitly disclosed within the relevant service offering. It is imperative to recognize that we do not govern, and hence do not assume responsibility for, other ways in which your personal information might be utilized by the third-party social media provider. We advise you to review their privacy policies to comprehend how they gather, employ, and distribute your personal data, and to establish your privacy preferences on their online platforms and applications.

Protection of Personal Information:

Safeguarding Personal Data Our commitment lies in safeguarding your personal data through a framework of organizational and technical security protocols.

We have implemented appropriate and rational technical and organizational security measures to ensure the protection of the personal data under our processing purview. Notwithstanding our security endeavours and measures to fortify the safeguarding of your information, it is imperative to acknowledge that no electronic data transmission over the Internet or storage technology is entirely impenetrable. Hence, we are unable to assure or guarantee complete invulnerability against potential threats posed by hackers, cyber criminals, or unauthorized third parties who may attempt to circumvent our security protocols to illicitly gather, access, pilfer, or modify your data. While we exert our utmost efforts to shield your personal information, the transfer of such data to and from our Services carries inherent risks that should be acknowledged. Therefore, it is advisable to access our Services only within secure environments.

Cookies:

Cookies, which are small pieces of data stored by web servers on web browsers, are used to enhance user experience. While both permanent and temporary cookies are placed on your computer's hard drive, they do not contain personally identifiable information. Third-party service providers may also employ cookies on the platform. Users have the option to accept or decline cookies, with the understanding that declining may limit access to certain platform features.
